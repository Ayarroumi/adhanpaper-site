Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Adhan Paper is a companion app for a physical e-paper prayer time device. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected and how it is used.

Information We Collect

The app may collect or process the following information:

  • Account information, such as the Google account identity used to sign in.
  • Authentication identifiers, such as Firebase user IDs and app backend user IDs.
  • Location information, such as city, country, latitude, and longitude, to calculate prayer times and configure the device.
  • Device and notification information, such as Firebase Cloud Messaging tokens and generated app device IDs, to support push notifications.
  • Purchase information, such as Google Play purchase records, purchase tokens, product IDs, and purchase status, to provide marketplace access and handle refunds.
  • App settings and device configuration information needed to sync the app with the Adhan Paper physical device.

How We Use Information

We use this information to:

  • Provide app login and account functionality.
  • Configure prayer times and device settings.
  • Sync settings with the physical Adhan Paper device.
  • Provide marketplace purchases and purchase history.
  • Send app or device-related notifications.
  • Maintain app security, prevent abuse, troubleshoot issues, and improve app functionality.

Data Sharing

The app uses third-party services such as Google Sign-In, Firebase, Google Play Billing, and cloud backend services. Data may be processed by these services as needed to provide authentication, notifications, purchases, and app functionality.

We do not sell personal data.

Data Security

User data sent to our backend, Firebase, and Google services is transmitted using encrypted HTTPS connections where applicable. Local communication with the physical Adhan Paper device may occur over local Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, or local network connections for device setup and sync.

Data Retention

We retain account, purchase, notification, and configuration data for as long as needed to provide the app, meet legal or accounting obligations, process refunds, prevent abuse, and maintain security. Some records may be retained for a limited period after deletion requests where legally or technically required.

Account and Data Deletion

Users can request deletion of their Adhan Paper app account and associated data by following the instructions on the Account and Data Deletion page.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@adhanpaper.com.
