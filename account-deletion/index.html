Adhan Paper

Account and Data Deletion

Adhan Paper allows users to sign in with Google to use the companion app for the Adhan Paper physical e-paper prayer time device.

How to Request Deletion

To request deletion of your Adhan Paper app account and associated data, email support@adhanpaper.com with the subject Account deletion request.

Please include the email address used to sign in to the Adhan Paper app.

What Will Be Deleted

After receiving your request, we will delete your Adhan Paper app account data where deletion is legally and technically possible. This may include:

  • App user profile and backend account records.
  • Stored device notification tokens.
  • App settings stored on our backend.
  • Device configuration records stored on our backend.
  • Purchase or marketplace access records where deletion is legally and technically possible.

What May Be Retained

Some data may be retained for a limited period where required for:

  • Security and fraud prevention.
  • Legal compliance.
  • Accounting and tax obligations.
  • Refund handling.
  • Google Play purchase verification.
  • Resolving disputes or abuse reports.

Deleting your Adhan Paper app account does not delete your Google account.

Contact

For account deletion or data deletion questions, contact support@adhanpaper.com.
